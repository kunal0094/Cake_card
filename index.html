Chocolate Cake


Preparation Time: 45 minutes Serves: 8

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix sugar and butter together.
  3. Add eggs and mix well.
  4. Combine dry ingredients and add to the mixture.
  5. Pour in the milk and stir.
  6. Pour batter into a greased pan.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes.
